We've been Making The Delicious Foods for 15+ years

We are a mom & pop's restaurant featuring Vietnamese cuisine. We have operated for more than 15+ years in the restaurant industry and love to serve our community. Some of the restaurants we opened in the past are Little Hot Plate near University of Washington, Bunsaigon in Everett, Mekong Village in Aurora to Meekong Bar in Belltown. Some of the food in Mekong Village will be included in the Cloud's menu. The Meekong Bar will continue to be operated by well experienced employees instead. With this, our family will be transitioning to Cloud Cafee. Many of our current employees from Mekong Village and Meekong Bar will be operating the Cloud Cafe with us.

We understand how much of an impact food can bring to the community culturally so, we are enthusiastic to bring the taste from Vietnam to the United States.

Our Vision

Restaurants are no longer just a place to celebrate important events;
they have become families' new dinner table. North Beacon Hill is eagerly
waiting for another Asian restaurant. The number of Asian restaurants, namely
Vietnamese restaurants are low and we will make a great addition. We satisfy the
classic and savvy connoisseurs who are looking to try something
new and exciting. We keep a contemporary yet elegant vibe in our service,
interior design, and presentation of our food.
